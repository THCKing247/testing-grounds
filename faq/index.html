Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services, pricing, process, and what to expect when working with us.

What services do you offer?

We specialize in custom web applications, automation & integrations, websites & landing pages, and technical consulting. Our services range from simple website builds to complex internal tools and workflow automation systems.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on project scope, complexity, and timeline. We provide custom quotes after understanding your specific requirements. Most projects range from a few thousand to tens of thousands depending on the work involved. Contact us for a detailed estimate.

How long does a typical project take?

Timeline depends on project complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while custom applications can take 6-12 weeks or more. We work in milestones and provide regular updates, so you'll always know where things stand.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Node.js, Python, and various cloud platforms. We choose the best stack for each project based on requirements, scalability needs, and your team's preferences.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. This can include bug fixes, updates, security patches, feature enhancements, and technical support. We can discuss support options that fit your needs.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in integrations and can connect new solutions with your existing tools, databases, APIs, and workflows. We'll work within your current tech stack when possible.

What's your development process?

We follow a structured process: Discovery (understanding your needs), Build (milestone-based development with feedback loops), Launch (testing and deployment), and Support (ongoing maintenance). You'll have regular check-ins and opportunities to provide feedback throughout.

Do you handle hosting and deployment?

Yes, we can handle hosting setup and deployment. We work with various hosting providers and cloud platforms. We can also help you choose the best hosting solution for your needs and budget.

What if I need changes after launch?

We're happy to make changes and enhancements after launch. We can work on a project basis for larger changes or set up a retainer for ongoing updates. We'll discuss post-launch options during the project.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our contact form or email us at info@apextsgroup.com. Include details about your project, timeline, and any specific requirements. We'll respond within 24 hours to discuss your needs.

Still have questions?

Get in touch and we'll be happy to discuss your project in detail.
